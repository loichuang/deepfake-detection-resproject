2. ldm_try_2.py

Image (512x512) → Frozen VAE Encoder → z → Add Noise → 20-step DDIM Denoising Loop → z^ → Calculate |z - z^| → Downsized MLP → Real/Fake.

①introduced a real DDIM Scheduler for a complete 20-step denoising loop
②changed the feature calculation to the absolute difference |z - z^| to force the MLP to focus on the magnitude
③used the mean of the latent distribution instead of .sample() during VAE encoding to remove random noise
④upgraded to 512x512 and used the actual CLIP text encoder
